Welcome to AirClip support. We're here to help you get the most out of your clipboard manager.

Getting Started

AirClip is a powerful clipboard manager for macOS that helps you manage your clipboard history efficiently.

  • Access your clipboard history anytime
  • Sync across devices with iCloud
  • All data stored locally and securely

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my clipboard history?
Use the menu bar icon or keyboard shortcut to quickly access your clipboard history.

Is my data secure?
Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect any information.

How do I enable iCloud sync?
Go to Settings and enable iCloud sync. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud on your Mac.
